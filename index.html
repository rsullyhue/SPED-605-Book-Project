Welcome

Finding out your child needs Augmentative and Alternative Communication (AAC) can feel overwhelming. You're probably wondering where to start, what device to choose, how to pay for it, and whether your child will actually use it.

This guide was created to help parents navigate the AAC journey. Whether you're just beginning to explore AAC or you're already in the thick of implementation, you'll find practical information, real strategies, and answers to common questions.

You are not alone in this. Thousands of families have walked this path before you, and many have found that AAC opened up a whole new world of communication for their child.
